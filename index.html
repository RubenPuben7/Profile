👋 About Me

I have close to five years of experience across HR analytics, change management, and systems implementation, with a background spanning both generalist and specialist HR roles. My experience sits at the intersection of people, systems, and data, which has developed a strong interest in how these areas can work together to support better decision-making and improve organisational outcomes.

Throughout my previous roles, I worked with workforce and payroll data across multiple systems to improve reporting accuracy, streamline processes, and provide insights to support organisational initiatives and operational decisions. This included developing Power BI dashboards, analysing employee engagement survey data, and preparing workforce reporting that informed initiatives related to workforce planning, DEI, employee experience, and organisational change.

Working across both HR and data has shaped a practical and insight-driven approach to problem solving. I’m naturally curious and enjoy exploring new ways of doing things, seeking different perspectives, challenging assumptions, and continuously improving how data is used to inform outcomes. I have developed an appreciation for the importance of accuracy and attention to detail when working with data, particularly where it supports decision-making and communication across teams and stakeholders.

Motivated by this interest, I completed a Graduate Certificate in Data Science and am currently enrolled in a Master of Science in Health Data Science, pending commencement in September 2026. Alongside formal study, I continue to build my analytical capability through university and personal projects using R and Power BI, with a focus on statistical analysis, data visualisation, and reporting. I also enjoy working through complex or unfamiliar problems and see continuous learning as a core part of my development.

This site highlights a selection of my projects, experience, and ongoing learning as I continue building my skills in analytics, reporting, and data-driven problem solving.
